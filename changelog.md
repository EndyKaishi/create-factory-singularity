## [0.1.0] - Automation Foundations

### Added
- crafting recipes for:
  - Allthemodium Alloy Sword
  - Allthemodium Alloy Pickaxe
  - Allthemodium Alloy Axe
  - Allthemodium Alloy Shovel
  - Allthemodium Alloy Trident
  - Allthemodium Alloy Paxel

- Mixing recipe to duplicate Soul Lava using:
  - 0.5 bucket Soul Lava
  - 0.5 bucket Lava

- Industrial chain for Ancient material production:
  - Stone can be haunted into Blackstone
  - Blackstone + Soul Lava can be mixed into Ancient Stone
  - Ancient Stone can be crushed into:
    - Allthemodium Nuggets
    - Vibranium Nuggets
    - Unobtainium Nuggets

- Mixing recipes for:
  - Ancient Dirt
  - Ancient Grass
  - Ancient Fern
  - Ancient Soulberries

- Crushing recipe for ore doubling.

- Mixing recipes for late-game alloy creation:
  - Allthemodium–Vibranium Alloy
  - Unobtainium–Allthemodium Alloy
  - Unobtainium–Vibranium Alloy

### Notes
Rare resources are now part of a gated automation chain:

Stone → Blackstone (Haunting)  
Blackstone + Soul Lava → Ancient Stone (Mixing)  
Ancient Stone → Rare Nuggets (Crushing)

Soul Lava must be obtained before rare material scaling becomes possible.

This establishes the first fully industrialized rare resource loop while preserving progression depth.
